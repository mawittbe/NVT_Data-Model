Ontology Specification Draft

Abstract

Linked-Data ontology for modelling cultural heritage information in the domain of performing arts focused on non-verbal form of theatre. The ontology development is part of the "NONVERBALES THEATER IM EHEMALIGEN OSTTEIL BERLINS 1970-1991" digitisation project at the Media Library for Dance and Theatre of the German Centre of the International Theatre Institute. The project is funded by the Förderprogramm Digitalisierung von Objekten des kulturellen Erbes Berlin of the Forschungs- und Kompetenzzentrum Digitalisierung Berlin (digiS).
