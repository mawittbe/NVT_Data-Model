Abstract

Linked-Data ontology for modelling cultural heritage information in the domain of performing arts focused on non-verbal form of theatre. The ontology development is part of the "NONVERBALES THEATER IM EHEMALIGEN OSTTEIL BERLINS 1970-1991" digitisation project at the Media Library for Dance and Theatre of the German Centre of the International Theatre Institute.
